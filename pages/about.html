Meet Dr. Wall

Dr. Breauna C. Wall

University of Minesotta Twin Cities

Dr. Breauna C. Wall stands as a distinguished figure in the education sector, with a career spanning over a decade, characterized by a blend of academic excellence and practical leadership. Breauna holds a Doctor of Education from Johns Hopkins University, a Master’s in Educational Leadership - Principalship from Northern Arizona University, and a Master’s in Special Education from Arizona State University, reflecting a deep and diverse academic foundation.

As a Clinical Practice Supervisor and Lecturer at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, Breauna imparts extensive knowledge to students across various credential and master’s degree programs. This role emphasizes the alignment of educational practices with California’s P-12 content standards, fostering diversity, inclusion, and the use of advanced educational methodologies and technologies.

Beliefs

With a distinguished career in special education, Dr. Wall stands at the forefront of facilitating transformative professional learning experiences. Specializing in empowering school leaders to enhance supervision of special education programs and staff, she is deeply committed to improving culture, communication, staff satisfaction, and educational outcomes. Dr. Wall’s work significantly focuses on bridging the divide between general and special education departments, equipping general education teachers and staff with the necessary skills and understanding to foster an inclusive learning environment.

Supervising and developing special education programs across 20 schools has allowed Dr. Wall to gain invaluable insights into the challenges and triumphs of creating effective educational strategies. Her expertise is backed by multiple special education credentials, comprehensive research in the field, and advanced graduate studies. This blend of practical experience, scholarly pursuit, and dedication to excellence in education enables Dr. Wall to deliver impactful professional learning initiatives.
