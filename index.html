Dr. Breauna C. Wall

Stands as a distinguished figure in the education sector, with a career spanning over a decade, characterized by a blend of academic excellence and practical leadership. Breauna holds a Doctor of Education from Johns Hopkins University, a Master’s in Educational Leadership - Principalship from Northern Arizona University, and a Master’s in Special Education from Arizona State University, reflecting a deep and diverse academic foundation.

Dr. Wall offers a comprehensive guide for K–12 school leaders, in her new book called Leading Special Education, focusing on supporting special education teachers and fostering successful programs through collaboration, professional learning, and innovative technology.

Commitment to Special Education

Empowering school leaders and bridging the gap between general and special education is not just a profession; it's a passion.

Dr. Wall stands at the forefront of facilitating transformative professional learning experiences. Specializing in empowering school leaders to enhance supervision of special education programs and staff, she is deeply committed to improving culture, communication, staff satisfaction, and educational outcomes.

Our work significantly focuses on bridging the divide between general and special education departments, equipping general education teachers and staff with the necessary skills and understanding to foster an inclusive learning environment.
